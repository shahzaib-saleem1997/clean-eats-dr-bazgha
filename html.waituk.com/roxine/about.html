Clean Eats By Dr Bazgha

Wellness,I came to realise,will not happen by accident.

Longevity should not just be about living longer . One must understand how to live better for longer.

Health. Fitness. Longevity

There is a strong connection between maintaining good health, prioritising wellness and extending one’s lifespan. By adopting healthy habits, and making conscious choices to take care of our bodies and minds, and keeping in mind that Sleep,Mental well-being,Exercise and Nutrition are key components of our Wellness journey, we can increase our chances of living a longer, more fulfilling life.

ABOUT OUR ONE ON ONE NUTRITION PROGRAM

The program would help you get rid of your ‘dieting’ mentality by educating you what ‘Healthy eating’ actually is and how,years and years of yo-yo dieting disrupts our hormonal balance of the body and makes weight management impossible.It will help you heal your gut and help to flourish and restore the gut microbiome


  • One on one support for a closely monitored personalised Nutrition program
  • Teaches you the fundamentals of healthy eating to manage your weight and balance your hormones
  • Teaches you how to eat mindfully without stripping yourself of the joy of eating
  • Helps you to achieve long term and sustainable results, keeping in mind your gut health,hormones and food psychology

    • WHO WOULD BENEFIT FROM OUR PROGRAM

  • If you have been caught up in the viscous cycle of yo yo dieting over the years and now find it very difficult to lose that stubborn weight .
  • If you are looking for weight management to improve fertility and increase your chances of conception
  • Post natal nutrition advice
  • If you are forcing yourself to eat ‘diet’ food, that is stripping the joy from your life
  • If you are suffering from gut issues or hormonal imbalances ( in the form of PCOS, Diabetes, Endometriosis,Thyroid) and do not understand how to make proper food choices.
  • If you are sick and tired of following one fad diet after the other and are looking for a proper long lasting solution to your weight management
  • If your constipation is worsening over time and laxative just aren’t helping any more
  • If you are pre or post menopausal and want to invest in your future health and wellness
  • If you are trying to sweat your weight off by doing hours of cardio but are just not succeeding

    • OUR PROGRAM INCLUDES

  • Regular Blood work suggestions & Report analysis
  • Recommendation for SUPPLEMENTS, if necessary
  • Highly personalised meal plans
  • Nutrition education
  • Continuous Whatsapp support
